A man fakes his death – level 1

一个人伪造自己的死亡--第1级

16-11-2022 15:00

Arthur Knight is from the US. In 2008, he rapes a woman. Later that year, he attacks another woman. A court says that Knight is guilty.

阿瑟-奈特来自美国。2008年，他强奸了一名妇女。同年晚些时候，他袭击了另一名妇女。一个法庭说，奈特是有罪的。

In 2020, Knight fakes his own death. He runs away to Scotland. He pretends to be Nicolas Rossi, a man from Ireland.

2020年，奈特伪造了自己的死亡。他逃到了苏格兰。他假装是尼古拉斯-罗西，一个来自爱尔兰的人。

Last year, Knight becomes ill. He has the coronavirus. He goes to hospital. Hospital staff see tattoos on Knight’s arms. Then authorities know who the man really is.

去年，奈特生病了。他得了冠状病毒。他去了医院。医院工作人员看到奈特手臂上的纹身。然后当局知道这个人到底是谁。

US authorities ask Scotland to return Knight to the US. This process is called extradition. Knight will return to the US in March 2023.

美国当局要求苏格兰将奈特送回美国。这个过程被称为引渡。奈特将于2023年3月返回美国。


A man fakes his death – level 2

一个人伪造自己的死亡--第2级

16-11-2022 15:00

On Friday, a Scottish court decided that Arthur Knight faked his own death, and his real name was Nicolas Rossi. Rossi will return to the US where a court convicted him of a sexual attack.

周五，苏格兰法院裁定，亚瑟-奈特伪造了自己的死亡，他的真名是尼古拉斯-罗西。罗西将返回美国，那里的法院判定他犯有性攻击罪。

In 2020, Rossi faked his own death, because he didn’t want to go to prison, and he escaped to Scotland. Last year, he got sick with the coronavirus and went to hospital. Staff at the hospital noticed that he had tattoos on his arms.

2020年，罗西伪造了自己的死亡，因为他不想进监狱，他逃到了苏格兰。去年，他得了冠状病毒病，去了医院。医院的工作人员注意到，他的手臂上有刺青。

Authorities learned that the man was someone else. For 11 months, Rossi tried to convince authorities that he was Knight. He said that someone tattooed his arms when he was unconscious in the hospital.

当局了解到，这个人是另一个人。11个月来，罗西试图让当局相信他是奈特。他说，当他在医院昏迷时，有人在他的手臂上纹身。

Rossi will return to the US in March 2023.

罗西将于2023年3月返回美国。


A man fakes his death – level 3

一个人伪造自己的死亡--第3级

16-11-2022 15:00

Nicolas Rossi spent 11 months trying to trick Scottish courts. He wanted them to believe that he was Arthur Knight, an Irish orphan who’d never been to the US.

尼古拉斯-罗西花了11个月时间试图欺骗苏格兰法院。他想让他们相信他是亚瑟-奈特，一个从未去过美国的爱尔兰孤儿。

Authorities alleged that Knight was lying, and he was actually Rossi, a fugitive suspect who was fighting extradition to the US. On Friday, a court ruled that the man was lying, and he will now face extradition to the US in March 2023.

当局称，奈特在撒谎，他实际上是罗西，一个正在争取引渡到美国的在逃嫌犯。周五，法院裁定此人在撒谎，他现在将面临2023年3月被引渡到美国。

In 2008, Rossi allegedly raped a 21-year-old woman, and he was fighting an extradition request by US authorities. In the same year, he was convicted of sexually assaulting another woman. In 2020, he reportedly faked his own death, and fled to Scotland to escape sentencing.

2008年，罗西据称强奸了一名21岁的女子，他正在与美国当局的引渡请求作斗争。同年，他因性侵犯另一名妇女而被定罪。据报道，2020年，他伪造了自己的死亡，并逃往苏格兰以逃避判刑。

Last year, staff at a Scottish hospital spotted Rossi’s tattoos while they treated him for coronavirus. However, he claimed that someone tattooed him while he was unconscious in hospital. He spent the last two years insisting that he was a victim of mistaken identity.

去年，苏格兰一家医院的工作人员在为罗西治疗冠状病毒时发现了他的纹身。然而，他声称有人在他在医院昏迷时给他纹身。在过去的两年里，他坚持认为自己是一个身份错误的受害者。

Difficult words: orphan(孤儿),allege(据称), fugitive (逃亡者), suspect (嫌疑人), extradition (引渡), convicted (被定罪的), sexually (性爱), assaulting (攻击性), sentencing (判刑), coronavirus (冠状病毒).
