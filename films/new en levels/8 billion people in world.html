8 billion people in world – level 1

世界上有80亿人--第1级

18-11-2022 15:00

The world’s population is 8 billion. China and India have the most people.

世界人口为80亿。中国和印度的人口最多。

About 1.4 billion people live in each of them. Currently, China is the most populous country. This fact may change next year. Experts say that China’s population will start to fall.

约有14亿人生活在这两个国家。目前，中国是人口最多的国家。这一事实可能在明年发生变化。专家说，中国的人口将开始下降。

For many years, Chinese couples can have only one child. Then the population gets old. They need young people to help them.

多年来，中国夫妇只能有一个孩子。然后人口变老。他们需要年轻人来帮助他们。

Now, politicians want to change the situation. Since 2021, married couples can have three children. Many couples want to have only one child. More children cost too much money.

现在，政治家们想改变这种情况。自2021年起，已婚夫妇可以有三个孩子。许多夫妇想只生一个孩子。更多的孩子需要花费太多的钱。

In India, big cities are crowded. Travel is dangerous. Too many people are everywhere.

在印度，大城市很拥挤。旅行很危险。太多的人无处不在。

Every year, millions of people come to live in cities like Delhi or Mumbai. The cities don’t have enough work. 20% of people are without a job. They live in slums. Often, they don’t have water or electricity.

每年都有数百万人来到德里或孟买这样的城市居住。这些城市没有足够的工作。20%的人没有工作。他们住在贫民窟。通常，他们没有水和电。

Difficult words: population (all the people who live in a place or country), populous (a country or area where many people live), slums (an area of a city where poor people live).

难词：人口（住在一个地方或国家的所有的人），人口众多（一个有很多人居住的国家或地区），贫民窟（一个城市中穷人居住的区域）。


8 billion people in world – level 2

世界上有80亿人--第2级

18-11-2022 15:00

This week, the world’s population reached 8 billion. The two most populous countries are China and India, and more than 1.4 billion people live in each of them.

本周，世界人口达到80亿。人口最多的两个国家是中国和印度，每个国家都有超过14亿的人口。

Experts say that India will become the most populous country next year. A huge population brings many problems, especially for big cities. Every year, millions of people come to live in cities like Delhi or Mumbai, and public places and transport become too crowded or dangerous. More than 20% of people are without a job, and they live in slums, often without water or electricity.

专家说，印度明年将成为人口最多的国家。庞大的人口带来了许多问题，特别是对大城市而言。每年有数百万人来到德里或孟买这样的城市居住，公共场所和交通变得过于拥挤或危险。超过20%的人没有工作，他们住在贫民窟，经常没有水和电。

According to the UN, China’s population will start to fall next year. In 1980, the country started its one-child policy, when a couple could have only one child. Then China’s people started to get old, and few young people live there to help them. Currently, married couples can have three children, but many have only one child because more children are too expensive.

根据联合国的数据，中国的人口将在明年开始下降。1980年，该国开始实施独生子女政策，当时一对夫妇只能有一个孩子。然后中国的人民开始变老，很少有年轻人住在那里帮助他们。目前，已婚夫妇可以有三个孩子，但许多人只生一个孩子，因为更多的孩子太昂贵了。

Difficult words: populous (a country or area where many people live), slum (a very poor and crowded area of a city), policy (a plan, law, or rule of actions).

You can watch the original video in the Level 3 section.

难词：人口众多（一个有很多人居住的国家或地区），贫民窟（一个城市中非常贫穷和拥挤的地区），政策（一项计划、法律或行动规则）。


8 billion people in world – level 3

世界上有80亿人--第3级

18-11-2022 15:00

This week, the world population reached 8 billion. China is the most populated country, but India is likely to surpass China in 2023. This year, each of those countries counted more than 1.4 billion residents.

本周，世界人口达到80亿。中国是人口最多的国家，但印度有可能在2023年超过中国。今年，这些国家的居民人数都超过了14亿。

India is facing challenges of crowded transport and overcrowded public spaces, and the biggest cities are coping with surging numbers of new residents.

印度正面临着交通拥挤和公共场所过度拥挤的挑战，而最大的城市正在应对新居民数量激增的问题。

According to the UN, China’s population will start to shrink in the next year. In 2021, the country’s fertility rate was one of the lowest in the world.

根据联合国的数据，中国的人口将在明年开始缩减。2021年，该国的生育率是世界上最低的国家之一。

From 1980 to 2015, China’s strict one-child policy was in place to keep its population in check. In 2015, a two-child policy started so young people could help the rapidly aging population. In May 2021, Beijing announced a three-child policy for married couples. However, the cost is high for childcare for little children, and people don’t want to have children.

从1980年到2015年，中国实行了严格的独生子女政策，以控制其人口。2015年，开始实行二胎政策，以便年轻人能够帮助迅速老龄化的人口。2021年5月，北京宣布对已婚夫妇实行三胎政策。然而，照顾小孩子的费用很高，人们不愿意生孩子。

Difficult words: surpass (to be greater or better than something else), fertility rate (the average number of children born to a woman), in check (under control).

难词：超越（大于或优于其他事物），生育率（妇女生育的平均数量），控制（控制之下）。
