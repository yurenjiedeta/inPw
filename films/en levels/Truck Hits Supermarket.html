Truck Hits Supermarket – level 1

There is an incident in Irkutsk. It is a city in Siberia. A truck drives into a supermarket. A security camera films the moment. The truck is going quite fast. It seriously injures one shopper. The driver is uninjured. He says that the brakes do not work.

显示隐藏生词

Irkutsk：伊尔库次克（西伯利亚的主要城市，位于俄罗斯东部贝加尔湖西岸）

Siberia：西伯利亚

shopper：购物者;(商店的)顾客

brakes：刹车;制动器;车闸;阻力;障碍 v 用闸减速;刹(车)


Truck Hits Supermarket – level 2

Russian police released security camera video which shows the moment that a truck drove into a supermarket. The incident happened in Irkutsk, one of Siberia’s largest cities. The truck was going quite fast and seriously injured one shopper. The truck driver was unharmed. People say that he said that the brakes on the truck failed.

显示隐藏生词

unharmed：adj. 未受伤的;未受损害的


Truck Hits Supermarket – level 3

Russian police released security camera footage showing the moment that a truck ploughed into a supermarket in Irkutsk, one of Siberia’s largest cities. The truck was going quite fast and seriously injured one shopper. The driver, who was unharmed, reportedly said that the brakes failed.

显示隐藏生词

footage：n. 镜头;(影片中的)片段

ploughed：v. 犁(田);耕(地);翻(土)

reportedly：adv. 据说;据报道;据传闻
