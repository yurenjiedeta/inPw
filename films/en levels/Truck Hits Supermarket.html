Truck Hits Supermarket – level 1

There is an incident in Irkutsk. It is a city in Siberia.A truck camera films the moment.

在伊尔库茨克发生了一起事件。这是一个位于西伯利亚的城市。一辆卡车的摄像机拍下了这一时刻。

The truck is going quite fast. It seriously injures one shopper. The driver is uninjured. He says that the brakes do not work.

卡车的速度相当快。它严重伤害了一名购物者。司机没有受伤。他说，刹车失灵了。

显示隐藏生词

Irkutsk：伊尔库次克（西伯利亚的主要城市，位于俄罗斯东部贝加尔湖西岸）

Siberia：西伯利亚

shopper：购物者;(商店的)顾客

brakes：刹车;制动器;车闸;阻力;障碍 v 用闸减速;刹(车)


Truck Hits Supermarket – level 2

Russian police released security camera video which shows the moment that a truck drove into a supermarket. The incident happened in Irkutsk, one of Siberia’s largest cities.

俄罗斯警方公布了监控录像，其中显示了一辆卡车驶入一家超市的瞬间。该事件发生在西伯利亚最大的城市之一伊尔库茨克。

The truck was going quite fast and seriously injured one shopper. The truck driver was unharmed. People say that he said that the brakes on the truck failed.

卡车的速度相当快，严重伤害了一名购物者。卡车司机没有受到伤害。人们说，他说卡车的刹车失灵。

显示隐藏生词

unharmed：adj. 未受伤的;未受损害的


Truck Hits Supermarket – level 3

Russian police released security camera footage showing the moment that a truck ploughed into a supermarket in Irkutsk, one of Siberia’s largest cities.

俄罗斯警方公布的监控录像显示，在西伯利亚最大城市之一的伊尔库茨克，一辆卡车冲进一家超市的瞬间。

The truck was going quite fast and seriously injured one shopper. The driver, who was unharmed,reportedly said that the brakes failed.

卡车的速度相当快，严重伤害了一名购物者。据报道，司机没有受伤，他说是刹车失灵。

显示隐藏生词

footage：n. 镜头;(影片中的)片段

ploughed：v. 犁(田);耕(地);翻(土)

reportedly：adv. 据说;据报道;据传闻
