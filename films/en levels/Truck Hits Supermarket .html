Truck Hits Supermarket – level 1

There is an incident in Irkutsk. It is a city in Siberia. A truck drives into a supermarket. A security camera films the moment. The truck is going quite fast. It seriously injures one shopper. The driver is uninjured. He says that the brakes do not work.

显示隐藏生词

Siberia：西伯利亚

shopper：购物者;(商店的)顾客

brake：刹车;制动器;车闸;阻力;障碍 v 用闸减速;刹(车)
