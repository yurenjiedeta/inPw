Help for blind people – level 1

Some people are blind. Blind people can’t see well or at all. They can’t cross the street easily. They don’t hear bikes and electric cars.

有些人是盲人。盲人看不清楚或根本看不清。他们不能轻易过马路。他们听不到自行车和电动车的声音。

Mael Fabien watches these people. He wants to help them. He thinks that robots can help.

梅尔-法比安看着这些人。他想帮助他们。他认为，机器人可以帮助他们。

Fabien gets an idea. He thinks about autonomous cars and how they work. Autonomous cars drive without a driver. The cars see obstacles.

法比安有了一个想法。他想到了自主汽车和它们的工作方式。自主汽车在没有司机的情况下行驶。这些汽车可以看到障碍物。

Fabien and other people make a special device. A person wears it around his neck. The device checks if obstacles are there. The device makes a noise to show the obstacles. A blind person knows where the things are.

法比安和其他人制作了一个特殊的装置。一个人把它戴在脖子上。该设备检查是否有障碍物。该设备会发出声音来显示障碍物。盲人知道这些东西在哪里。

People can buy the device in September 2022. It’ll cost $3,150

人们可以在2022年9月购买该设备。它将花费3150美元

Help for blind people – level 2

A Swiss company developed a device which helps people who are partly or completely blind.

一家瑞士公司开发了一种设备，帮助部分或完全失明的人。

One of the company founders and CEO is named Mael Fabien. He got the idea about the device when he saw that some people couldn’t easily cross the street when bikes or electric cars passed them. They couldn’t hear them well.

该公司的创始人和首席执行官之一名叫Mael Fabien。当他看到一些人在自行车或电动车经过时不能轻易过马路时，他就有了这个装置的想法。他们不能很好地听到它们。

Fabien thought about robots which could already solve these problems. He wanted to use similar technology to autonomous cars. Then the idea was born to make the device.

法比安想到了已经可以解决这些问题的机器人。他想把类似的技术用于自主汽车。然后就有了制造这个设备的想法。

The device sends sounds to the user and when an obstacle is in the way, the sounds get louder. People can buy this device in September this year, and it’ll cost $3,150.

该设备向用户发送声音，当有障碍物挡住去路时，声音会变大。人们可以在今年9月买到这个设备，它的价格是3150美元。

Help for blind people – level 3

A Swiss-based startup has developed a smart harness that supports the visually impaired with navigation. The device is called Biped One, and it uses the same technology for autonomous cars.

一家位于瑞士的创业公司开发了一种智能安全带，支持视力障碍者的导航。该设备被称为Biped One，它采用了与自主汽车相同的技术。

It guides pedestrians with 3D sounds that become louder near obstacles. The sounds play into a pedestrian’s headphones as he moves. The device finds obstacles, cars, pedestrian crossings, and depending on the situation, it keeps important information.

它用3D声音引导行人，这些声音在接近障碍物时变得更响亮。当行人移动时，这些声音会在他的耳机中播放。该设备可以找到障碍物、汽车、行人横道，并根据情况保留重要信息。

The company’s co-founder and CEO said that he saw many visually impaired people who couldn’t easily cross the street if bikes or electric cars passed them. He thought about how some robots could already do this task, and then he developed his project. Biped One goes on sale in September 2022, and it’ll cost $3,150.

该公司的联合创始人兼首席执行官说，他看到许多视力受损的人，如果自行车或电动车从他们身边经过，他们就不容易过马路。他想到了一些机器人已经可以完成这项任务，然后他就开发了自己的项目。Biped One于2022年9月上市，售价3150美元。
